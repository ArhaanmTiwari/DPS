The library of DPS Indore stands as a cornerstone of the school's commitment to academic excellence and holistic education. It features an extensive collection of books, including academic texts, fiction, non-fiction, periodicals, and digital resources, ensuring that students have access to a wealth of knowledge. The library is a modern, well-equipped space with comfortable reading areas, computer access, and dedicated librarian support, fostering a conducive environment for learning and exploration. It actively promotes a culture of reading through various programs such as book clubs, author visits, and reading challenges. By supporting research, project work, and information literacy, the DPS Indore library plays a pivotal role in enhancing the educational experience and intellectual growth of its students.

The DPS school auditorium stands as a central hub of cultural and educational activities, embodying the institution's commitment to holistic development. Equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual systems, comfortable seating, and a spacious stage, it provides an ideal setting for a wide range of events including school assemblies, theatrical performances, musical concerts, and guest lectures. The architectural design prioritizes acoustics and visibility, ensuring every attendee has an immersive experience. The auditorium also serves as a venue for inter-school competitions, fostering a spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition among students. Its multifaceted use underscores its role in enriching the educational journey, offering students a platform to showcase their talents and gain confidence in public speaking and performance.

Indore World Summit 2022

Academic Gains with Global Perspective through MUN

The young and enterprising delegates of DPS Indore attended the Indore World Summit 2022 organised by Muniversiti and AFS India. The three day conference comprised four delegates of the school as the Secretariat members.

The event witnessed an enthusiastic participation of 120 DIPSITES of classes VI-XII, along with more than 1600 students from across the country. Students solved real-life problems in simulation of the UN or the Parliament in the form of 24 committees over the course of the conference from October 15-17, 2022.

All the world’s a stage,
And all the men and women merely players;
They have their exits and their entrances;
And one man in his time plays many parts,
His acts being seven ages.

These beautiful lines written by William Shakespeare remind us that life is a
performance. We all have a role to play, and it is up to us to decide how we want to play
it. Shakespeare was baptized on April 26, 1564 and he died on April 23, 1616. Shakespeare,
the poet, dramatist, and actor, often called the English national poet “was not of an age, but
for all time”. To commemorate his contribution in the field of English literature, DIPSITES
celebrated Shakespeare Week at DPS Indore , by decorating the corridors with his words
and images, thereby creating imaginative visions of pathos and mirth seen in the works of
this literary legend. Even today, his mental energy, when applied to intelligible human
situations, finds a memorable expression that is astonishingly convincing and
imaginatively stimulating. William Shakespeare 03
